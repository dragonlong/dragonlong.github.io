About

Xiaolong Li (pronunced as /shiao-long-lee/) 李小龙

I am a second-year Direct Ph.D. student in computer engineering at Virginia Tech. I'm currently working in the center of Advanced Research Computing, advised by Prof. A. Lynn Abbott. My primary research direction is computer vision and deep learning.

Before that, I worked on biomedical image processing with Prof. Yunhui Zhu and Prof. Guohua Cao in the X-Ray Systems Lab.

I gained my bachelor degree in Huazhong University of Science and Technology, leading the team on algorithms development and system integration for drone control, and 3D Reconstruction at the Mechanical Innovational Base(MIB) of Qiming College.
