About

I am now in my second year of Ph.D. program in the ECE department at Virginia Tech. I work in the 3D Optical Information Group and I am advised by Professor Yunhui Zhu. I gained my bachelor degree in Huazhong University of Science and Technology. My general fields of interest are computer vision on drones, digital imaging processing, machine learning and parallel computation. Currently I am doing research about quantitative phase imaging, non-convex optimization, which is a collaborated project with X-ray System Lab in Virginia Tech.
